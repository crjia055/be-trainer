How shadowing works
Step 1 — Listen 2–3 times at normal speed. Don't try to repeat yet. Let the rhythm and stress sink in.
Step 2 — Record yourself saying the phrase immediately after. Don't pause to think — speed matters.
Step 3 — Play both back to back. Your ear is the judge. Listen for rhythm and stress, not just individual sounds.

Most learners find round 3 sounds noticeably closer to the model than round 1. That improvement is the mechanism.
